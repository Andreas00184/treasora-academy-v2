Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Treasora Academy ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices when you use treasoraacademy.com and related services, including Dominar AI, lessons, and your Financial Passport.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Learning data: lesson progress, quiz results, certifications, and Financial Passport responses.
  • Dominar conversations: questions you ask and responses provided, used to personalize your experience.
  • Payment information: if you subscribe to Pro, payment is processed by Stripe; we do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Usage data: device type, browser, pages visited, and general analytics to improve the platform.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide and improve Treasora Academy, personalize Dominar's guidance, track your learning progress, process Pro subscriptions, send service-related emails, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal information.

AI & Dominar

Dominar uses your Financial Passport, lesson history, and conversation context to tailor educational responses. Dominar provides financial education only — not personalized investment advice. Do not share sensitive identifiers (Social Security numbers, full account numbers) in chat.

Data Sharing

We may share data with trusted service providers (hosting, authentication, payment processing, AI infrastructure) who are bound to protect your information. We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our users and platform.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account data by contacting us at support@treasoraacademy.com. You can cancel a Pro subscription at any time from your account settings.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure authentication. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children

Treasora Academy is intended for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@treasoraacademy.com or visit our Contact page.
